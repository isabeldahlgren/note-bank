\section*{Polynomials}
Polynomials show up in every field of mathematics.
\begin{definition}[Polynomial]
    Polynomials are algebraic expressions on the form $$a_0 + a_1 x + a_2 x^2 + ... + a_n x^n$$
\end{definition}
